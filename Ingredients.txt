Pasta Primavera Recipe. Ingredients: tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, garlic, olive oil, pasta. Instructions: Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Add onions and garlic, sauté until fragrant. Add bell peppers and tomatoes, cook until they soften. Cook pasta separately. Toss cooked pasta with the vegetable mixture. Serve hot. Enjoy!

Chocolate Chip Cookies. Ingredients: butter, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, flour, baking soda, chocolate chips. Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F. Cream together butter, sugar, and brown sugar. Add eggs and vanilla extract, mix well. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop dough onto a baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Let cool and enjoy your cookies!

Guacamole. Ingredients: avocados, lime juice, onion, tomatoes, cilantro, salt, pepper. Instructions: Cut avocados and remove the pit. Mash avocados in a bowl. Add lime juice, chopped onion, diced tomatoes, and chopped cilantro. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything well. Serve with tortilla chips or as a dip for tacos. Delicious!

------------------------------

Mango Salsa. Ingredients: mangoes, red bell pepper, red onion, fresh cilantro, lime juice, salt, jalapeño pepper (optional). Instructions: Dice the mangoes, red bell pepper, and red onion. Chop fresh cilantro finely. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the mixture. Add a finely chopped jalapeño pepper for some heat (optional). Season with salt to taste. Serve with grilled chicken or as a topping for fish tacos.
